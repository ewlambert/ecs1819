Computer Science By Luke Patton-Van Buskirk

Computer Science is a track that is dedicated to developing both software and hardware, and robotics. There are many courses that Lane offers. Examples include, AP Computer Science A (which covers a whole semester of CS in college), Robotics, Media Computation, and a class dedicated to teaching you business with Computer Science. This course also isn't just talk, they also have preformed with robots on a stage live, have their own art project with 3D printing in a museum, and have had Tim Cook in the building before.

They also have many electives, so you can learn many things with the Computer Science course. If you want to learn how to program websits, you can go ahead and do that. If you would rather learn about programming servers and softwares for computers, you can go ahead and do that.

Overall, Computer Science is one of the many electives Lane has to offer, and it's a very good one in my opinion.
