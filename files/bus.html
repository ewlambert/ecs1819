Bus

There are 140 bus routes in Chicago. Because Chicago has been blessed with a great transportation system, you can get anywhere within the city! There are many bus stops located around lane. There is the Addison bus and the Western bus. The Western bus has an express bus while the Addison bus does not. This is because the Western street is very long. It runs all across Chicago. In order to get people faster from one side of the city to the other they made an express bus. Addison is not as long so there is no need for an express bus. The bus stops that are most commonly taken to arrive at Lane Tech are the 152 buses or 49 and X49. The 152 bus, or Addison bus, is better for people who live far out south and near Lake Shore Drive, also people that live up north closer to the school. Where u live determines if you get on the 152 that goes toward Lake Shore or the one that goes in the directions of Cumberland. Many students take the Western bus or in other words the 49 or X49, when going to school, or home from school. The X49 is a quicker way to get to school because it doesn’t stop at all of the bus stops, unlike a regular Western bus. Not everyone can take an express Western bus because it might not go to the stop that you need to get off at. Either bus that you take will lead you to and from school. Going home from Lane some people have the option to choose which bus they want to take out of these two because they can get home either way. The best way is to find what route is best for you, and won’t take you a while to get home. Visit the page about student Ventra cards, this will help you find out how to get the reduced fare for the bus and train.
