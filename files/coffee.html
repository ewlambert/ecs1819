Coffee by Isabella Korellis

There are three places around Lane that students can get coffee from. These places are Starbucks, Dunkin Donuts and 7-Eleven. Not every kid at Lane drinks coffee, but it is nice to know the different places so you can also stop by to get snacks and a drink. 7-Eleven is the farthest away and has the smallest variety of coffee. It is a convenience store, but they have a coffee machine where you serve yourself. The main things in the store are snacks and cheap food. The second farthest place from Lane is Starbucks. This is probably the most expensive of all of the choices. They are a standard coffee place that also has a selection of food. They have baked foods and healthier options, like sandwiches. Lastly, Dunkin Donuts which is closest to Lane, it is directly across the street on Western. The prices are very cheap and they have a very similar coffee menu to Starbucks.
