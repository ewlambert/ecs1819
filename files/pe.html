PE/Health - Allegra Coleman

Requirements

As a Freshman and Sophomore you are required to take one semester of PE, and then second-semester health for Freshman and Drivers Ed for Sophomores. As a junior or Senior, you could take a different type of PE class such as Adaptive PE, Dance, Fit Female, Group Exercise, or Weight Training. You don’t necessarily have to take a PE class as a junior or senior as you only need two credits of PE but there are options if you're not interested in the other electives.

Advice

In the semester of PE, you will take a 5-week course of swimming, meaning you will need to change into and out of your swimsuit. I suggest wearing a simple outfit to put your bathing suit under so changing into your swimsuit won’t be hard and you can put on your outfit easily too cause you'll only get 10 minutes to change. Make sure use a separate bag for your stuff and a plastic bag to put your wet swimsuit in when you're done swimming. Use two swimsuits so switch out so one can dry while the other is in use. During regular PE I still suggest wearing a simple outfit so changing isn't a hassle, also keep your uniform in either you're locker or backpack along with your shoes and take it home every weekend to wash to be ready for the next week. Always keep the essential ones you for after PE such as lotion and deodorant.
